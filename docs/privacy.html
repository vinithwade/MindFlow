Privacy Policy — MindFlow

Last updated: 2026-06-02

MindFlow ("the app", "we") is a macOS desktop app that turns your voice plus what's on your screen into a polished, ready-to-send reply. This policy explains what data the app handles and where it goes.

What the app processes

  • Microphone audio (transient). When you hold the shortcut, audio is recorded and sent to your selected speech-to-text provider for transcription. Raw audio is not stored after transcription.
  • On-screen content (transient). To ground a reply, the app reads the text you're replying to — via your selection, macOS Accessibility, or (only if you enable it) a screenshot processed by on-device OCR. This is sent to your selected AI model to generate a reply.
  • Generated replies & transcripts. Stored locally; if you sign in, synced to your account across devices.
  • Account info. If you create an account, we store your email and (optionally) name.
  • Settings. Stored locally and, if signed in, synced. API keys are encrypted on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Third-party services

Depending on your configuration, data may be sent to: OpenAI/Anthropic (reply generation, OpenAI Whisper for transcription), Deepgram (transcription), Supabase (auth + sync), and Google (only if you choose Google sign-in — email + basic profile). We send only what's needed for the requested action.

Storage & retention

Local data stays on your Mac until you clear it or delete the app. Synced data is retained until you delete it or delete your account. Deleting your account removes your synced replies, settings, and profile.

Your choices

Use the app without signing in (no sync); disable screenshot OCR (off by default); clear local history, export your data, or delete your account from the app's Account settings.

Security

API keys are encrypted at rest using the OS keychain. Network requests use HTTPS. Per-user data is isolated with row-level security.
